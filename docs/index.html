Letter from Paul Siple to Mr. Behrend

About the image

This letter from Paul Siple to Mr. Behrend informs us of his journey to Antarctica. We learn that they traveled to a volcano by Mt. Raymound Foadick in the Marie Byrd Land. We also learn more about who all went on this trip with Mr. Siple. This includes Alton Wade, Geologist, Stevenson Corey, Olin Stan-cliff, and drivers and assistants. We also learned that Mr. Behrend also lent him money and tools for the expedition such as a sextant described in the letter.
